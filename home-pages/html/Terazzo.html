Why Terazzo Laminate Flooring?

Simply put, it’s an ultra-durable product that looks like a real wood, yet costs about one-third less than the real wood. It is easier to install and typically lasts longer.

Installation & Maintenance


Terazzo is as close to “maintenance-free” as a floor can get. The high pressure laminate surface makes it virtually impossible for dirt or grime to get a foothold.
We have created the Terazzo certified Installer program to give the customers a flawless installation to bring back the ethnic and artistic look. Terazzo Certified Installers are given three- month training programme on installation. Terazzo Experienced Certified Installers are specially trained and certified to expertly install any Terazzo flooring product. Terazzo Tested Certified Installers must pass a rigorous hands-on and written exam to become certified.
